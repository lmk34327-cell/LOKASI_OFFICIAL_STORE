# How to Add Your Own Product Images

So you've made your own designs? Here is how to put them on the website.

## Step 1: Save Your Image
1.  Take your photo or design file (JPG or PNG).
2.  Rename it to something simple (no spaces).
    *   Example: `my_new_tee.png` or `red_hoodie.jpg`
3.  Copy the file into the `mockups` folder inside your `LOKASI_OFFICIAL_STORE` folder.
    *   Path: `Documents/LOKASI_OFFICIAL_STORE/mockups/`

## Step 2: Add to Website Code
1.  Open the file `data.js` (you can open it with Notepad or a code editor).
2.  Find the `defaultProducts` list.
3.  Copy one of the existing blocks (everything between `{` and `}`).
4.  Paste it at the top of the list.
5.  Change the `image` part to match your new filename.
    *   Change: `image: "mockups/old_image.png"`
    *   To: `image: "mockups/my_new_tee.png"`
6.  Update the `name`, `price`, and `desc` (description) as needed.

## Example
```javascript
{ 
  id: 999, 
  name: "MY NEW DESIGN", 
  price: 599.99, 
  category: "TOPS", 
  gender: "UNISEX", 
  tag: "FRESH", 
  image: "mockups/my_new_tee.png", // <--- THIS IS THE IMPORTANT PART
  desc: "Description of your new fire drip.", 
  colors: ["#000000"] 
},
```

## Step 3: Refresh
Refresh your website (F5) and the new item will appear!
